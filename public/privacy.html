Privacy Policy

Last updated: 4 May 2026

This privacy policy describes how DepOversight (“we”) handles personal information collected through the marketing site at depoversight.com.

What we collect

The early-access form on this site asks for:

  • Work email (required)
  • Company, GitHub organisation, package ecosystem, current scanner tool — all optional
  • A free-text message — optional

We also use a single localStorage key (depoversight.lang) to remember the language you selected. We do not set tracking cookies. We do not run third-party analytics.

How we use it

Information you submit through the early-access form is used only to:

  • Reply to you about early access to DepOversight.
  • Understand which dependency ecosystems and existing tools early-access requesters are using, so we can prioritise integrations.

We do not sell or share your data. We do not send marketing email. We do not use your information to train machine-learning models.

How long we keep it

Early-access submissions are retained until you ask us to delete them or until DepOversight reaches general availability and we close the early-access programme, whichever comes first.

Your rights

You can email us at any time to:

  • See what data we hold about you.
  • Correct it.
  • Have it deleted.

For requests under GDPR, UK GDPR, CCPA, or similar regimes, contact us and we will respond within 30 days.

Contact

Email hello@depoversight.com for any privacy question or request.

Changes to this policy

If we materially change how we handle data, we will update the “Last updated” date above and, if you have submitted the early-access form, notify you by email before the change takes effect.
